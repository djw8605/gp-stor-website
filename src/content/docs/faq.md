# FAQ

## Who can use GP‑STOR?

Projects affiliated with institutions in the Great Plains Network and communities such as NRP, OSG, and ACCESS programs. Broader access policies will be published with the production launch.

## What types of data are appropriate?

Research data intended for computation, analysis, or sharing via supported integrations. No HIPAA, FERPA, or other restricted or legally regulated data unless explicitly approved.

## How much storage can I request?

Allocation policies are being finalized. Expect guidance for both short‑term high‑speed tier use and longer‑term object storage.

## What does it cost?

Using GP-STOR is free of charge for research and educational purposes, subject to allocation policies.

## How do I get support?

Training materials, virtual office hours, and contact channels will be advertised on this site. For now, consult your campus CI lead.
