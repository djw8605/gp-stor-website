## Collaborating Institutions

The GP-STOR project builds on successful regional collaborations and partnerships across the Great Plains Network, including:

- University of Nebraska-Lincoln
- University of South Dakota
- University of Missouri
- Great Plains Network (GPN) member institutions

## Related Projects

GP-STOR extends and enhances prior NSF investments:

- **GP-CT** (Great Plains CyberTeam) - NSF #1925681
- **GP-ARGO** (Great Plains Augmented Regional Gateway to the Open Science Grid) - NSF #2018766
- **GP-ENGINE** (Great Plains Extended Network of GPUs for Interactive Experimenters) - NSF #2322218
