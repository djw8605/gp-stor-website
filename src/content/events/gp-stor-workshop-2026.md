## GP-STOR Beta User Workshop

Join us for an interactive workshop designed to onboard our first wave of beta users to the GP-STOR platform.

### What You'll Learn

- How to access GP-STOR through various methods (Globus, Nextcloud, NRP mount)
- Best practices for organizing and managing your research data
- Performance optimization tips for different workload types
- Q&A with the GP-STOR engineering team

### When & Where

**Date:** March 15, 2026  
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM CST  
**Location:** University of Nebraska-Lincoln (Hybrid event with virtual attendance option)

### Who Should Attend

This workshop is designed for:
- Beta users who have been granted early access
- Research computing staff interested in storage infrastructure
- Principal investigators planning data-intensive projects

### Registration

Registration details will be announced in early 2026. Space is limited for in-person attendance.

### Prerequisites

For hands-on portions of the workshop, you'll need:
- A laptop with SSH client
- Basic familiarity with command-line interfaces
- Your GP-STOR access credentials (provided upon beta acceptance)
