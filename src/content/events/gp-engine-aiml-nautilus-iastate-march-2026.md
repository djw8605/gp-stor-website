## GP-ENGINE: Migrating AI/ML workflows to Nautilus

This hands-on workshop will teach participants how to migrate AI/ML workflows to the Nautilus platform, part of the [National Research Platform (NRP)](https://nationalresearchplatform.org/). Attendees will learn about containerization, Kubernetes basics, and practical strategies for running AI/ML workloads on distributed computing infrastructure.

**When:** March 17th, 2026, 12:00 pm to 3:00 pm (Tentative)  
**Location:** Iowa State University (In-person)  
**Cost:** FREE!!!  
**Host:** This event is hosted by the [GP-Engine](https://gp-engine.org/) project.

### Learning Objectives

- Understanding the National Research Platform and Nautilus
- Introduction to containerization for AI/ML workflows
- Kubernetes fundamentals for researchers
- Hands-on practice migrating AI/ML workloads to Nautilus
- Best practices for resource management and optimization
- Troubleshooting common issues

### Format

This session will be delivered in an in-person format at Iowa State University.

*Attendees will need a computer capable of a web connection as this workshop will feature plenty of hands-on practice.*
