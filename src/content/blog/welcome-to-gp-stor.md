We're excited to announce the Great Plains Scalable Tiered Object Repository (GP-STOR), a distributed storage infrastructure designed to accelerate research across the Great Plains region.

## What is GP-STOR?

GP-STOR provides 14 PB of redundant Ceph object storage plus 384 TB of high-speed solid-state storage, specifically designed for AI/ML streaming, simulation output, and instrument data.

## Key Features

- **Three-Site Resilience**: Storage resources distributed across the University of Missouri, University of Nebraska, and University of South Dakota
- **Multiple Access Methods**: OSG and National Research Platform mounts, OSDF Origin nodes, Globus endpoints, and web-based UI via Nextcloud
- **Training and Support**: Virtual office hours, video walkthroughs, and comprehensive guides
